How much can drivers make with Drivio?

The money you make with the Drivio app depends on when, where, and how often you drive.

How earnings are calculated

Standard fare

You earn a fare for every completed trip.

Surge

Drive during peak demand to earn more per trip.

Minimum trip earnings

Each city has a minimum guaranteed for trips.

Service fee

This fee helps fund app development and support.

Cancellations

You may earn a fee if a rider cancels after requesting.

Promotions and how they work

In-app promotions help you earn more by driving when demand is high.

These promotions include incentives like:

  • Surge Pricing: Get paid more when demand increases.
  • Quest Rewards: Complete a set number of trips to earn bonuses.
  • Boost Zones: Earn more in specific areas during peak hours.

Stay updated in the app to take full advantage of these offers.

Some ways to earn

Getting ahead with the app

The app shows where you're likely to get rides.

Getting tipped for your service

You keep 100% of your tips directly from riders.

When and how you get paid

Cash out fast: Weekly transfers to your bank account.

If your customer pays cash: Get paid immediately after trips.
